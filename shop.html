They go to the shop

All three Nibbles,Blaze and Vixey head to the shop. As they arrive the fox family greets Nibbles, and Blazes' parents apologise for breaking the staff.

While waiting for Blaze and his father make 2 different staffs, Nibbles asks a question to Vixey.

He questions how Blaze got that scar by his eye. Vixey answers by saying that when he was 14 and she was 16, she went to get something from the back a badger came trying to steal a sword.

Blaze ended up fighting him and the badger stabbed him close to his eye, but luckly a ranger bear helped Blaze.

After talking to Vixey and playing with Blazes' siblings Blaze and his father finish making the staffs' for Nibbles.

Nibbles has a hard time choosing a staff.

The first one has a piece of his old staff, light-weight, with a place for the cheese to be placed surrounded by metallic shapes that resemble grasshopper legs.

The second one is more metalic, a bit more heavy but twice as powerful, with a place for the cheese.
